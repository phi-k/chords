# Chords — Project Philosophy

> **Chords is a free music tool, available to everyone, and we hope it will remain so forever.**

The following commitments are not legal obligations. They represent the founding values of the Chords project and the intentions of its creator.

## Our values

The author commits to ensuring that the **official version** of Chords:

- **Remains free and ad-free.** No paywall, no subscription, no ads — ever.
- **Does not collect any personal data.** Your music, your privacy.
- **Remains a tool at the service of musicians**, without commercial compromise.

## For forks and derived versions

Chords is licensed under the [AGPL-3.0](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.html), which grants broad freedoms — including commercial use and UI modifications.

We encourage anyone who forks or extends Chords to share these values, but **we do not impose them**. The license speaks for itself.

## Contributing

If you share this philosophy and want to help, contributions are welcome on the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/phi-k/chords). Whether it's code, translations, bug reports or ideas — every contribution matters.
